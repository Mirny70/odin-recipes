Pasta recipe


Description

This pasta recipe is a quick and easy dish that can be made in under 30 minutes. It's perfect for a weeknight dinner or a weekend lunch. The combination of fresh vegetables and pasta makes it a healthy and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta of your choice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish
  • Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and saut√© for 1 minute.
  3. Add bell pepper and zucchini, and cook for 5 minutes until softened.
  4. Add cherry tomatoes and cooked pasta to the skillet. Season with salt and pepper, and toss to combine.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.
